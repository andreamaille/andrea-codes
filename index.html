About Me

Hello! My name is Andrea Maille and I’m a front-end web developer living in Toronto, ON. My journey into web development all started with my Etsy Store when I wanted to build my own website. One google search ended up with me finding my dream career.

I've always been a creative person and when I discovered coding, I realized I had discovered the ultimate creative experience. I love seeing art, music, sound, movement, design and creative thinking all come together to create new spaces online. With every new project, I love writing organized, clean and reusable code that engages users through responsive design, subtle movement and artistic flairs.

When I’m not coding, you can catch me listening to k-pop or late 90’s pop punk, playing cello, catching up on my favourite podcasts or journaling in my many notebooks.
